Server Guidelines

  • Respect Everyone: Treat your fellow schoolmates with respect. No harassment, bullying, or hate speech.
  • No Griefing: Do not destroy or modify builds that do not belong to you without permission.
  • No Cheating: The use of hacked clients, x-ray mods, or exploits to gain an unfair advantage is strictly prohibited.
  • Keep it School-Appropriate: Chat and builds must remain appropriate for a school environment.
